HA LONG - QUANG NINH

Immerse yourself in the spectacular splendor of Ha Long, Vietnam, where majestic limestone cliffs meet turquoise waters, welcoming you on an extraordinary trip of breathtaking landscapes and cultural attractions.

Ha Long Bay

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a natural wonder of Vietnam, captivating travelers with its stunning emerald waters, mystical limestone karsts, and hidden caves

Sunworld Ha Long park

It is a thrilling amusement park nestled in the heart of Ha Long Bay, where visitors can enjoy an array of exhilarating rides, captivating shows, and immersive entertainment experiences

Tuan Chau Island

Located in the mesmerizing Ha Long Bay, is a tropical paradise that offers a range of attractions and activities, including pristine beaches, water sports and luxurious resorts.

Vehicles

plane

You can fly directly to Van Don International Airport, which is the closest airport to Ha Long, ensuring a convenient and time-saving journey.

Car or motorbike

You can choose to drive by car or motorbike, enjoying the scenic routes and the flexibility to explore surrounding areas.

Bus

Taking a bus offers you an affordable and comfortable way to reach Ha Long, with various bus services available from major cities in Vietnam.

Hotel

Luxury Level: The Vinpearl Ha Long Bay Resort

Is a top-tier luxury hotel, offering opulent accommodations, world-class amenities, and breathtaking views of Ha Long Bay.

Mid-range Level: The Novotel Ha Long Bay

Is a popular choice for travelers seeking a comfortable and affordable stay, which offers well-appointed rooms, modern facilities, and a range of amenities.

Budget Level

the City Bay Palace Hotel provides affordable accommodations without compromising on comfort,, which offers clean and cozy rooms, friendly service, and easy access to nearby attractions

Plans for 3 days in Ha Long

Day 1 : Explore Ha Long Bay

Taking a boat tour to HaLong Bay, where you'll witness the iconic limestone karsts and breathtaking landscapes.

Visiting the mesmerizing Thien Cung Cave (Heavenly Palace Cave) to marvel at its intricate stalactite formations.

Enjoy a relaxing afternoon on a traditional junk boat, cruising along the bay and soaking in the stunning scenery.

End the day with a memorable sunset view over Ha Long Bay.

Day 2: Thrills at Sunworld Ha Long Park

Begin your day at Sunworld Ha Long Park, enjoying a wide range of exhilarating activities from roller coasters to water slides

Catching entertaining live shows and performances throughout the day

Taking breaks to savor delicious local cuisine at the park's restaurants

Wrapping up the day with a stunning night-time experience, as the park illuminates with colorful lights and a lively ambiance.

Day 3: Relaxation at Tuan Chau island

Starting with a leisurely morning on its pristine beaches, enjoying the sun, sand, and tranquil waters.

Engaging in various water sports activities such as kayaking, jet-skiing, or taking a scenic boat tour around the island.

Explore the Tuan Chau Pearl Farm to learn about the pearl cultivation process and purchase unique souvenirs.

Indulge in local seafood delicacies at one of the island's restaurants, savoring the flavors of Ha Long Bay.

End your trip with a peaceful sunset stroll along the beach
